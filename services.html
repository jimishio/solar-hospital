Solar Hospital Pvt Ltd

Solar Hospital located opposite to AEC Office, Naranpura is a 35 bedded Multispecialty Hospital with the facilities of 2 Modular OTs, 10 Bedded ICU with Isolation facility, Special Rooms, Semi-Special Double Sharing Rooms, Semi-Special Triple Sharing Rooms and General Ward. OPDs, Radiology, Pathology facilities.

Solar Hospital was inaugurated by the Honorable President of B.J.P., Shri Amitbhai Shah on the Auspicious day of Rath-Yatra, 25th June, 2017. On the occasion, Solar Hospital was blessed by Shri Shankarsinh Chaudhary: Honorable Health Minister, Gujarat; Shri Gautambhai Shah, Mayor, Ahmedabad; Shri Bhupendrabhai Patel: AUDA Chairman; Shri Dineshbhai Desai Chairman: Health Department, Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation; Shri Parbatbhai Patel: Ex Health Minister, Gujarat.

Neurology

Neurology Department is managed by Team of Neuro Physicians and Surgeons. 24x7 care is provided by the team. Solar Hospital has emerged as a Successful Trauma Centre of the area because of successfully giving positive results in Trauma and Complex Cases by its Combined Care of Neurophysicians, Neurosurgeons, Ortho Surgeons and Team.

Orthopedic Surgery

Solar Hospital feels proud of having the Renowned Orthopedic Surgery Team headed by Dr. Yakshat Shah. The surgeries like Joint Replacements, Arthroscopy, Spine Surgeries, Trauma Surgeries. Solar Hospital in a very short period has emerged as a very efficient and cost effective Joint Replacement and Trauma Centre due to the Specialized and Dedicated Ortho-Team.

Cosmetic Surgery

Dr Harshvardhan Sahijwani is a Plastic, Cosmetic & Microvascular Surgeon with Fellowship in Aesthetic (Cosmetic) Surgery from Belgium. Dr Harshvardhan is currently focussed in the field of Cosmetic and Reconstructive Plastic Surgery at Solar Hospital.


Cardio-Thoracic & Vascular Surgeries

Cardio-Thoracic and Vascular Surgeries are led by Leading CVTS of Gujarat, Dr. Bhupesh D. Shah, Dr. Abhishek Parmar and Team. Dr. Subhash Chaudhary offer the Cardiology consultancy at Solar Hospital.

Critical Care

24 hours Intensivists Services leaded by Dr. Rajesh Desai and Dr. Parul Gaadhe makes Solar Hospital ICU a very Safe Place for the Critical Patients in the Safe Hands of Experts. Solar Hospital ICU has back-up of all the High End Facilities of Dialysis, Ventilators, Monitoring, etc.
